FAQ

• Why to report ADR?

 • Moral responsibility of healthcare professional

 • Safeguard public health

 • Reduce economic burden on patient and government due to ADR


• Who can report ADR?

 • Healthcare professionals

 • Non-Healthcare professionals

 • Consumers

 • Pharmaceutical companies(to NCC)


• What to report?

 • All types of suspected ADR (known or unknown, serious or non-serious, frequent or rare)

 • ADR associated to conventional, alternative and traditional medicine systems

 • Specific fields of interest for ADR reporting: pregnancy, lactation, paediatric an geriatric

 • ADR due to lack of efficacy, overdose, antibiotic resistance and errors(spurious and misbranded drugs)

 • ADR related to abuse, off-label use and occupational drug exposure
